Interior Design

Interior design is a profession of taste and elegance, most of the time designers face the trouble of showing their ideas to the client in an imaginable way. It is my job to deliver not only the form, but also the mood of the design to the client. Eventually, I am required to add my own touch of colors, lighting and textures to the 3D shots to provide the desired mood. The visualization process keeps me up-to-date to what is new in the interior design field while working in parallel in my architectural career. It also improves my 3D modeling skills since most of the times I am required to model complex pieces of furniture. Which in return keeps me engaged with the most intimate scale of design.
