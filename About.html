About Khalid

An enthusiastic Architect who believes that if there is only one skill to be acquired in life, it will be the ability to receive and comprehend knowledge. Graduated from the Architectural department, Faculty of engineering, Cairo university in 2010 with highest distinction degree of honor ranking the 2nd on his class. Later, he Finished his Masters of Science in Architectural Engineering in 2016 from Cairo University. Since his graduation in 2010, he thrived to maintain a balance between the academic life and the practical one. As a result, he was one of the co-founders of I.El-Habrouk architects with its owner Arch. Ismail El Habrouk in 2011 beside his work in Cairo University as a teachin assistant. He contributed in various architectural and interior design projects and competitions as an outsource for reputable architectural firms and independent architects. He believes that Teaching Architectural design and visual skills in Cairo university - both semester based and credit hour system programs- improved his leadership capabilities and presentation skills. That is why he always encouraged his students to criticize his teaching methods so he can always know how to reach them faster and more efficiently. Now, Khalid is settled in Berlin, working as a project architect in a reputable architectural firm in Germany. Besides architecture, he seeks knowledge in various fields of art including music, graphic design, painting in different medias, 3D modeling and 3D presentation.
